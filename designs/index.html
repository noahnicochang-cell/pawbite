How to pick

1.

Open all 7 in new tabs. Skim them — first impression matters most.

2.

Pick the one that makes you feel "yes, that's PawBite." If two are close, tell Claude which one and what to borrow from the other.

3.

The winner becomes the template for every other page in the site (PDP, Learn, About, etc).
