An essay

On the gut, and why we started here.

Most dogs are walking around with chronic low-grade digestive issues. Not the kind you take them to the vet for — the loose stool that comes and goes, the gas after dinner, the itchy skin that no shampoo really fixes. A lot of it traces back to the same place: the gut. So that's where we start.
